Summary


  • Born 28 December 1937
  • Education - Cornell University
  • Ratan Tata received the Padma Bhushan in 2000
  • Recieved Padma Vibhushan in 2008
  • Won the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year in Business
  • In 2008 Tata oversaw Tata Motors' purchase of the elite British car brands Jaguar and Land Rover from the Ford Motor Company.
  • The following year the company launched the Tata Nano, a tiny rear-engined
  • He gained experience in a number of Tata Group businesses and was named director in charge (1971) of one of them
  • He became chairman of Tata Industries a decade later and in 1991 succeeded his uncle
  • Tata aggressively sought to expand it, and increasingly he focused on globalizing its businesses
  • In 2000 the group acquired London-based Tetley Tea for $431.3 million
  • in 2004 it purchased the truck-manufacturing operations of South Korea's Daewoo Motors for $102 million
